How to Order

  1. Browse the menu and choose your favorite dishes.
  2. Click on the item to add it to your order.
  3. Review your order and confirm the selected items.
  4. Enter your contact and delivery details.
  5. Select your preferred payment method.
  6. Submit your order and wait for confirmation.

How to Reserve a Table

  1. Go to the Contact or Reservation page.
  2. Fill in your full name, email, and phone number.
  3. Select the date and time for your reservation.
  4. Enter the number of guests.
  5. Add any special requests (optional).
  6. Click the "Submit Reservation" button.
  7. Wait for a confirmation email or phone call.
